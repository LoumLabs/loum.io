Keyboard Shortcuts

Deck A

  • 1 + mouse: Level (double-tap to reset)
  • q + mouse: High EQ (double-tap to reset)
  • a + mouse: Mid EQ (double-tap to reset)
  • z + mouse: Low EQ (double-tap to reset)
  • d: Cue
  • f: Play/Pause
  • g: Stop
  • h: Pitch Down
  • j: Pitch Up

Deck B

  • 2 + mouse: Level (double-tap to reset)
  • w + mouse: High EQ (double-tap to reset)
  • s + mouse: Mid EQ (double-tap to reset)
  • x + mouse: Low EQ (double-tap to reset)
  • c: Cue
  • v: Play/Pause
  • b: Stop
  • n: Pitch Down
  • m: Pitch Up

Crossfader

  • space + mouse: Adjust
  • Double-tap space: Center
